About Us

VéloParis was founded in 2018 with a clear vision: to transform urban mobility in Paris through sustainable and accessible cycling solutions. We are a team passionate about cycling, technology, and environmental responsibility, dedicated to making Paris a greener and more connected city.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide a sustainable, accessible, and elegant mobility alternative that enables residents and visitors to explore Paris in an eco-friendly way, reducing carbon emissions and promoting a healthier lifestyle.

🌍

Our Vision

To become Europe’s leading cycling mobility platform by 2030, expanding our model to 50 cities and actively contributing to the creation of cleaner, healthier, and more livable urban environments for everyone.
